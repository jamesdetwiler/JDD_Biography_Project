About Me

I currently serve as a chaplain in the United States Air Force and an Online Teaching Assistant for The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary. I earned a B.S. in Political Science from the University of Kentucky, a Master of Divinity from Gateway Seminary, and a Ph.D. in Christian Preaching from The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary. With over two decades of pastoral, teaching, and leadership experience, I have served in a variety of cultural settings and ministry contexts. My wife, Meredith, and I married in 2004. We met while serving as short-term missionaries in Hawaii, China, and Thailand. In my spare time, I enjoy pursuing my interests in music, sports, literature, and cooking.
